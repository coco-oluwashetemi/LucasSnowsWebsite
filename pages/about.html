Born From the Love of Winter

The Story Behind Snow Trails

Snow Trails was founded in 1965 with a simple vision: to bring together people who love the magic of winter. What began as a grassroots effort a few friends carving trails and sharing their passion quickly grew into a dedicated agency for winter enthusiasts.

By the late 1970s, we had expanded beyond slopes, offering guided tours, training programs, and curated winter experiences that connected adventurers with the season in new ways. The 1980s and 1990s marked our transformation into a true company: we built partnerships with resorts, launched specialized programs for families and athletes, and introduced events that celebrated the culture of snow sports.

Entering the 2000s, Snow Trails embraced innovation, investing in modern equipment, professional coaching, and sustainable practices to ensure winter adventures could thrive for generations. By 2010, we had become more than a ski area we were a trusted agency, hosting winter festivals, torchlight parades, and family traditions passed down through generations.

Today, after more than half a century, Snow Trails stands as a global hub for winter enthusiasts a place where history, expertise, and passion come together to craft unforgettable experiences in the snow.

Your Community for Snowy Adventures

Built for winter enthusiasts, inspired by adventure. We believe in the power of the outdoors to bring people together and create lasting memories on the snow.

We also believe in the spirit of innovation blending tradition with modern experiences to help skiers, snowboarders, and families discover new ways to enjoy winter.

We’re committed to helping our community embrace the season fully, by crafting experiences that spark joy, strengthen connections, and celebrate the magic of snow year after year.

Hello, and thank you for visiting Snow Trails. Since taking the lead in 2010, I’ve had the privilege of guiding this company through an incredible journey of growth, adventure, and community. Winter has always been more than a season to me it’s a feeling, a tradition, and a chance for people to connect with nature and with each other. My goal has always been to make that experience accessible to everyone who shares that love for the snow.

Over the years, I’ve worked with an amazing team to expand what Snow Trails offers introducing curated winter experiences, meaningful family events, and programs designed for both seasoned adventurers and first-time explorers. Every decision we make is rooted in respect for the outdoors, the joy of discovery, and the belief that winter can be a magical experience for all ages.

As you explore this website, I hope you feel the passion, dedication, and care that goes into everything we do. Whether you’re planning your first winter getaway or returning for a tradition you hold dear, we’re here to help you create moments that stay with you for a lifetime. Winter is more than a season it's a way of life, and I’m grateful to share that journey with you.

— Lucas Snow Founder & Director, Snow Trails
